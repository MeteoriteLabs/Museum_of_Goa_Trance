7. METHODOLOGY NOTES
A. Hard Data Used Directly (No Modification)
• Goa tourism statistics
• Foreign tourist numbers
• Charter flights
• Tourism employment data
• Boom Festival economic report
These figures are used exactly as published.
---
B. Modeled Estimates (Clearly Labeled)
Used only where global public data is fragmented:
• Global trance festival economy
• Ozora & similar festival economic ranges
• Lifetime cumulative global impact
All modeled values are:
• Conservative
• Rounded downward where uncertain
• Based on publicly available attendance & ticket ranges
• Do not include inflated sponsorship estimates
• Do not include underground/unreported economy
---
C. Economic Modeling Assumptions
Used across estimates:
Average major festival attendance:
10,000 – 40,000
Average ticket price:
$150 – $350
Tourism multiplier applied conservatively: 1.5x – 2.5x direct spend
All assumptions based on: published festival pricing
tourism economics studies
event impact reports
---
8. DEFENSIBILITY STATEMENT
All modeled figures in this document are presented as:
“Conservative estimates based on publicly available attendance, pricing, and economic impact studies.”
This ensures: credibility
policy defensibility
media reliability
Figures are intentionally conservative rather than exaggerated.
